Fine tuning was performed on the KoBART model using technology, science books, or papers. The total number of data is 19134, and the ratio of training and verification data is 8:2

The Korean text summary divides the text into paragraphs of appropriate size and summarizes them through a summary model. If you enter a required word, only the paragraph that contains the word will be used for the summary.

The pdf summary extracts the text of the pdf by paragraph and summarizes the paragraph using a summary model. If there are pictures in the pdf, print them out according to the order of paragraphs.
